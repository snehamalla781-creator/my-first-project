Momo

Ingredients: Flour, Water, Minced Meat (chicken/pork), Onion, Garlic, Ginger, Salt, Pepper

Steps:

  1. Make dough with flour and water, let it rest for 30 min.
  2. Prepare filling: mix minced meat with chopped onion, garlic, ginger, salt, and pepper.
  3. Roll dough into small circles and place filling in the center.
  4. Fold and seal the momos.
